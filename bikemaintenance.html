Bike Maintenance

Worcester Earn-A-Bike is a non-profit bicycle shop which prides itself in helping people learn proper bike maintenance. The shop offers free workshops which cover basic topics in bicycle maintenance.The shop also offers an Earn-A-Bike program which allows volunteers to earn a free bicycle in exchange for helping around the shop for a total of 10 hours. More information on Worcester Earn-A-Bike can be found on their website here: http://worcesterearnabike.org/
